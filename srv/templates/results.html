About Ranked Choice Voting

Ranked choice voting ensures the winning book has the broadest support:

  • If a book gets over 50% of first-choice votes, it wins immediately
  • Otherwise, the book with the fewest votes is eliminated
  • Votes for eliminated books transfer to voters' next choices
  • This continues until one book has majority support
